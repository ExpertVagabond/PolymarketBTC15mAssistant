Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

PolySignal ("we", "our", "the Service") is operated by Purple Squirrel Media LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our prediction market signal scanner and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you subscribe, we collect your email address for authentication and signal delivery.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics about dashboard usage, feature interactions, and signal engagement to improve the Service.

Telegram/Discord IDs: If you subscribe to alerts, we store your messaging platform user ID to deliver signals.

Payment Information: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers, CVVs, or full payment details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect Polymarket wallet addresses or trading activity
  • We do not track your positions, balances, or trade history
  • We do not collect financial or brokerage account information
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking

4. How We Use Information

  • Deliver signal alerts via your chosen channel (web, Telegram, Discord)
  • Authenticate your account and manage subscriptions
  • Improve signal quality and model accuracy through aggregated, anonymized usage patterns
  • Communicate service updates, outages, or policy changes

5. Data Storage and Security

Account and subscription data is stored in encrypted databases. Signal history is stored locally and is not shared with third parties. We use HTTPS for all web traffic and secure WebSocket connections for real-time data.

6. Third-Party Services

We interact with the following external services to provide the signal scanner:

  • Binance API: Cryptocurrency price and volume data (public market data only)
  • Polymarket CLOB/Gamma API: Prediction market prices and orderbook data (public market data only)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Telegram Bot API: Signal delivery to Telegram subscribers
  • Discord API: Signal delivery to Discord subscribers

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

7. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Signal history is retained for model improvement. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Unsubscribe from alerts at any time via bot commands

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
